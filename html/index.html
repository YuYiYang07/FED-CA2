Red Color

Red symbolizes luck, happiness, and warding off evil. It is used in decorations, clothing, and red envelopes to bring good fortune and protection, especially from the mythical beast "Nian".

Circular Shape

The circle represents reunion and completeness. It appears in round foods like dumplings and reunion dinners, symbolizing unity among family members during the festival.

Dragon Totem

The dragon is a symbol of power, prosperity, and protection. Dragon dances are performed to drive away bad spirits and welcome success in the coming year.
